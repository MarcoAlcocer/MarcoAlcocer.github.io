Marco Alcocer

Marco Alcocer

I am a Predoctoral Fellow at ITAM and Innovations for Poverty Action in Mexico City and PhD Candidate at the University of California, San Diego. Starting in fall 2023 I will be an Academy Scholar at Harvard University, after which I will be an Assistant Professor at the University of California, Merced.

I research crime, violence, and citizen security, with a particular focus on organized crime and policing. My research relies on original data on hard to measure phenomena and multi-method research designs.
